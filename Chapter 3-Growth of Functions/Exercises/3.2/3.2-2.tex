\textbf{Exercise 3.2-2:}\\
$
a^{\log_bc} = e^{\ln a \cdot \log_bc} = e^{\frac{\ln a \cdot \ln c}{\ln b}}\\
c^{\log_ba} = e^{\ln c \cdot \log_ba} = e^{\frac{\ln c \cdot \ln a}{\ln b}}\\
\therefore a^{\log_bc} = c^{\log_ba}\\
$
