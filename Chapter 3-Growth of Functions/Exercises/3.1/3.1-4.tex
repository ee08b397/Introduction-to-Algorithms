\textbf{Exercise 3.1-4:}\\
$
\because 2 ^ {n + 1} = 2 \cdot 2 ^ n\\
\therefore 2 ^ {n + 1} = O(2 ^ n)\\
\because 2 ^ {2n} = (2 ^ n) ^ 2\\
\therefore 2 ^ {2n} \ne O(2 ^ n)\\
$
